Privacy Policy

Arbahara respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data you share through this website, the donation flows, and the membership circle.

What We Collect

  • Contact information (name, email, phone) submitted through forms.
  • Financial dashboard screenshots (e.g., Zelle confirmations) shared voluntarily.
  • Interaction data such as which pages you visit and how you engage with membership stories.

How We Use It

The information helps us coordinate planning updates, deliver membership content, and comply with donor stewardship. We use collected emails for project newsletters, event notices, and occasional surveys about sanctuary design.

Third Parties

We do not sell your information. We may share summaries with project partners (architects, accountants) who help deliver the monastery. These partners are bound to keep the information confidential and use it solely for the purposes specified by Arbahara.

Security & Retention

We keep data for as long as is necessary to fulfill planning needs. Access to the information is limited to the inner team, and we use industry-standard safeguards to protect against unauthorized disclosure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data—just send an email to arbahara27@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.
