Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy covers how Arbahara collects, processes, and retains data through the SMS program, membership flows, and donation workflows.

What We Collect

  • Contact information such as name, phone number, and email submitted through our forms.
  • Data submitted via donation confirmations (e.g., Zelle receipts) when shared voluntarily.
  • Interaction analytics such as page views and engagement with membership announcements.

How We Use It

The information enables us to deliver planning updates, communicate membership reminders (via SMS and email), and coordinate fundraising efforts. We may also use contact details to notify you about new opportunities or changes to the Dallas campus plan.

Opt-Out Rights

You can opt out of the SMS program at any time by texting STOP to the number you received messages from. You can also request removal from the mailing list by emailing arbahara27@gmail.com.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share summary planning data with trusted partners (architects, financial advisors) strictly for project delivery, and they are contractually obligated to keep it confidential.

Security & Retention

Data is stored securely and retained only as long as needed for planning. Access is limited to the core team, and we use standard safeguards to prevent unauthorized disclosure.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data—simply email arbahara27@gmail.com. We aim to honor requests promptly and transparently.
