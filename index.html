Construction Timeline

  1. 2025 • Foundation & Pilgrimage

    Lay the structural slab, stonework, and pilgrim welcome hall with cream terrazzo floors and dark orange accent lighting.

  2. 2025 Q4 • Sanctum Assembly

    Raise the main sanctuary, install stained halo windows, set the nave lighting, and tune acoustics for Zema chanting.

  3. 2026 • Community Launch

    Open the sanctuary for Monday prayers, install the iconostasis, and schedule monthly diaspora gala dinners.
