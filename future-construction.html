Building on purchased land

Construction plan and site vision

Now that land has been purchased, this page should function less like a speculative “future construction” board and more like a real construction-vision page. It should explain what will be built, what has to happen first, and why construction-stage giving matters right now.

Planning Detail

Expanded Grounds Concept

What construction now means

  • The land must be prepared for long-term monastery development.
  • Engineering, layout, and utility planning must be completed before major building begins.
  • The first phase should establish worship and gathering space as early as possible.
  • Each build step should support a larger long-term monastery campus, not only a temporary structure.
  • Fundraising should be tied to real, visible construction progress on the purchased property.
Major build elements

What the property can grow into

Church and prayer space

A central liturgical space for worship, prayer, and the life of the monastery community.

Hospitality and gathering

Areas for teaching, fellowship, events, and welcoming supporters, families, and pilgrims.

Monastery infrastructure

Site access, utilities, support buildings, and the groundwork needed for long-term campus growth.
